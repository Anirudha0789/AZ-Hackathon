
There are XX people participating in a quiz competition and their IDs have been numbered from 11 to XX (both inclusive). Beth needs to form a team among these XX participants. She has been given an integer YY. She can choose participants whose ID numbers are divisible by YY. 
Now that the team is formed, Beth wants to know the strength of her team. The strength of a team is the sum of all the last digits of the team members’ ID numbers.
Can you help Beth in finding the strength of her team?
Input:

The first line of the input contains a single integer TT denoting the number of test cases. TT lines follow 
The first line of each test case contains XX and YY. 

Output:

For each test case print the strength of Beth's team

Constraints

1≤T≤10001≤T≤1000
1≤X,Y≤10201≤X,Y≤1020

Sample Input:
2
10 3

15 5
Sample Output:
18 

10
EXPLANATION:

Example case 1: ID numbers divisible by 3 are 3,6,9 and the sum of the last digits are 3+6+9=18
